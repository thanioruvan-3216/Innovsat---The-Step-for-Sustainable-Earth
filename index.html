INNOVSAT - The Step for Sustainable Earth

Overview

Innovsat is a climate-monitoring satellite designed to aid agricultural farmers and fishermen by tracking, analyzing, and communicating critical environmental data.

Functionalities of Innovsat

1. Climate and Weather Monitoring
2. Agricultural and Marine Alerts
3. Data Transmission and Alert System
4. Orbit and Coverage
5. Benefits for Sustainable Farming
